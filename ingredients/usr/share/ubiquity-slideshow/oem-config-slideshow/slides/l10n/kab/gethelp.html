Amuddu n ufus d tallelt

Tasemlit tuṣibt tegber ddeqs n yeḥricen imugan ɣef Ubuntu. Yella deg srid neɣ s tignit n tallelt deg Dock.

Deg Suter Ubuntu tzemreḍad testeqsid daɣen ad tnadiḍ ddeqs n tegrummiwin n yisteqsiyen i yesɛan yakan tiririyin. Tallelt s tutlayt-ik izmer ad tettunefk deg Tarbaɛt-ik Tadigant.

I tansiwin ɣer tiɣbula iwulmen, rzu maulacaɣilif ɣer Tarbaɛt n tallelt neɣ Tallelt s tedrimt.
