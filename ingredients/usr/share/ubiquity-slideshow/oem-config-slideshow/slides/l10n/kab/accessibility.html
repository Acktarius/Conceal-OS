Anekcum i medden akk

Deg ul n tfiluzufit n Ubuntu tella tmedlit d akken tasenselkimt tettwag i medden akk. S ifecka n unekcum yuẓan d iɣewwaṛen ad k yeǧǧen abeddel n tutlayt, tafelwit n yiniten d teɣzi n weḍris, Ubuntu yettarra tasenselkimt teshel i yal yiwen u anda ma telliḍ.
